FAQ

How do I receive daily verses?

Make sure you have notifications enabled in your device settings. You will receive a daily notification with a new Bible verse.

How do I get a blessing?

Press the "Get blessed" button on the home screen. You can receive one blessing per day.

How do I collect points?

You earn points by engaging with PrayDay features, such as receiving daily verses and blessings.

How do I avoid temptations using PrayDay?

Use the "Avoid Temptations" helper for practical advice and scripture-based support to help you overcome life's challenges.
